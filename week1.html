new interfaces (speculative design)

beetle

Beetle key: the bettle key has all the functions to a car within its figure. Like normal keys, it can be easily carried around in a pocket. However, another function it has it the "call feature" where you can call the bettle and it comes to where you are as it can move. The pinchers can be squeezed to lock your car, each leg can be switched to unlock your car doors, and squeezing the back two legs unlocks your boot, pressing the wings unlocks your car.

ball

Balled mouse: essentially, it is a mouse that is in the shape of a heavy, metal ball; it's functionalitise are a bit different to a normal mouse. As illustrated in the diagram, you roll the ball around to move your cursor around the screen. You click with the "balled mouse" by banging it on a surface, and you type with it by grasping it three times to activate "keyboard" mode and you type with "drag typing".

Key ball: you put the ball into a specifically designed ball-keyhole to unlock a door.
